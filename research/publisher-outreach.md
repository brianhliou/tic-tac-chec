# Publisher outreach

Status: draft for review; not sent.

## Objective

Ask Wood Expressions for the complete official rules of its Bobby Fischer
reissue. The current product page advertises "updated tournament rules," while
its "PDF Instructions" tab contains no document or download link. The 1998
solve remains an immutable, separately labeled result regardless of the reply.

## Contact-form draft

Hello Wood Expressions team,

I recently completed an open-source, exhaustive computer solution of the
original 1998 Dream Green edition of Tic Tac Chec. The game is a draw with
perfect play, and the interactive tablebase is available at
https://tic-tac-chec.brianhliou.com/.

Your Bobby Fischer edition is described as having updated tournament rules. I
would like to model and solve that edition separately, without assuming its
rules match the original. Would you be willing to send me the current official
rulebook or connect me with the person responsible for the rules? The "PDF
Instructions" tab on the product page currently appears empty.

If the rulebook does not address them, I would also like to confirm when
movement and captures unlock; the complete pawn rules, including first moves,
redeployment, board edges, and capture direction; any repetition, stalemate, or
draw rule; and whether the updated rules apply only to the reissue or are meant
to clarify the original edition.

I will preserve the original solve separately and clearly credit the official
rules. May I cite or quote your response in the public research record?

Thank you,
Brian Liou

## Follow-up handling

1. Save the reply date, correspondent's role, and exact wording before
   interpreting it.
2. Ask targeted follow-ups only for questions the supplied rulebook leaves
   unresolved.
3. Formalize the 2025 edition under a new rules identifier and test fixtures.
4. Solve and publish it as a separate artifact and write-up; do not replace the
   1998 tablebase or silently change the explorer default.
