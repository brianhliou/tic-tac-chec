# Piece credits

The explorer uses the **Cburnett** chess piece set by Colin M.L. Burnett,
licensed under GPLv2 or later. These eight SVGs are the pawn, knight, bishop,
and rook artwork embedded by
[`chessground`](https://github.com/lichess-org/chessground) 9.2.1 and used as
Mistboard's default chess piece set.

The solver source remains licensed under the terms declared in
`solver/Cargo.toml`. A distribution of the explorer that includes this artwork
must also satisfy the artwork's GPL terms.
